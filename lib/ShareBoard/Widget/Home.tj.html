ShareBoard is the proof of concept showcase of Syncler - a distributed backend for KiokuJS.

Syncler can characterized as the real-time, distributed, optimistic replication system, based on operations transfer. It keeps several replicas of arbitrary data synchronized, in real-time.

For persistency it can use any KiokuJS backend (though at the moment there's only one (for CouchDB), writing new ones is trivial).

As the transport, Syncler uses Socket.IO. Due to relative instability of many Socket.IO transports, Syncler work correctly only with websockets, so you may need to open this page in Chrome.
